Placement Cell

Rules and Guidelines for Students

1. Student should regularly visit the Placement Cell website for jobs/internships/training updates.

2. The interested students should register with the Placement Cell by filling the Student Registration From (below).

3. All registered students will receive an email about jobs/internships/events.

4. Placement Cell will allow only registered students of JNU to sit for the Campus Placement.

5. All the information related to Campus Recruitment/Jobs/Internships will be available on the Placement Cell webpage.

6. Placement Cell will not be responsible for absenteeism in any test, interview or any selection process that are required by the company.
